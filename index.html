Green Moment App 減碳時刻App

About 關於

99% of Taiwan's population uses the Taipower power grid. I monitor the Taipower grid every ten minutes, use it's current generation portfolio (coal: 1,000MW, solar: 2,000MW) to get the CO2e emission for a kwh generated on the Taipower grid for that ten minutes, and utilize ml to predict future carbon emissions to tell the user when to use electricity to emit less carbon. Surprisingly, no one has thought to do this before.

99%的台灣人口都使用台電的電網。我每十分鐘監測台電電網，用它當下的發電組合(燃煤: 1,000MW，太陽能: 2,000MW)來算出電網當下生產一度電排放多少CO2e(不同時間產生一度電的排電因發電組合不同會有高低)，並使用機器學習來預測電網排碳並告訴使用者何時用電會產生較低的碳排放。

Status: Almost done with the last ML part being migrated onto GCP.

狀態：接近完成，須將最後機器學習部份轉移至GCP雲端。

Uni Job and Rent Hunt Map 大學打工/租屋地圖

About 關於

At CCU, establishments post their job listing in FB communities and complain about posts easily being washed away by new posts and the complexity in reaching students. I created an interactive leaflet based websites to show students about the job listings from establishments near our school and peoples review about working there. Since the domain name is ccumap.com, I also created a map for students who is looking for places to rent, and let the two maps piggy back off of each other in terms of being remembered by students.

在中正大學，店家會將他們的職缺放在臉書社團的貼文中。他們時常抱怨貼文很容易被洗掉，很難接觸到學生。我做了一個以leaflet地圖為基底的互動式地圖，上面以點在地圖上呈現了各店家的質缺憾過去打工的人對他們的評價。同時，既然網域名是租用ccumap.com，我同時做了租屋地圖，讓單獨去看低依地圖的使用者也會注意到另一個地圖，增加地圖被使用的機會。

Home Server 個人伺服器

About 關於

A dedicated home server setup contributing computational resources to scientific research through distributed computing projects including cancer research, climate modeling, and COVID-19 studies.

專用家庭伺服器設置，透過分散式運算專案為科學研究貢獻計算資源，包括癌症研究、氣候建模和COVID-19研究。
